Red Rocks Amphitheatre · Morrison, CO Free admission
Drive & Park at Upper North Lot
GPS destination: 18300 W Alameda Pkwy, Morrison, CO 80465. Once inside the park, follow signs for the Upper North Lot / Top Circle Lot — this puts you right at the top entrance so you walk DOWN the stairs to your seats. Best sunrise vantage, zero uphill climbing. Park opens 1 hr before sunrise.
18300 W Alameda Pkwy, Morrison — Apple Maps
Confirm no concert at redrocksonline.com — non-event days are always free.
~6:31 AM
Sunrise at Red Rocks Free
The amphitheatre faces directly east toward downtown Denver — one of the most breathtaking sunrises in Colorado. Easy for family to join, no ticket needed, just show up.
7:00 AM
Visitor Center & Colorado Music Hall of Fame Free
Right next to where you parked — no need to move the car. Opens 7 AM. Exhibits on every legendary artist who has performed at Red Rocks. Worth 20–30 min, great for all ages.
~8:30 AM
Huckleberry Roasters Coffee · Visitor Center
Denver's award-winning coffee roaster inside the Visitor Center — right where you're parked. The perfect warm-up after sunrise. Opens with the Visitor Center at 7 AM.
9:00 AM+ optional
Trading Post Gift Shop & Trail
Located at the Trading Post building at the bottom of the park — a short drive down from the Upper North Lot. The gift shop has great Colorado souvenirs and Red Rocks gear. The Trading Post Trail is a 1.4-mile loop through the red rock formations starting right outside.
The trail and gift shop are a separate stop from the Visitor Center up top. If the crew is energized, head down — if everyone is ready for lunch, skip ahead. You've already had the full experience!
